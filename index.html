About me

I am Piyush Deshpande, pursuing Master's in Computer and Information Technology from Purdue University. I am passionate about automating manual processes and leveraging technology to assist individuals with disabilities. My journey began with a fascination for how visually impaired individuals navigate their surroundings and has evolved into a mission to develop impactful assistive technologies.

In my professional roles at IDeaS Revenue Solutions and Coforge Limited, I honed my skills in C++, Java, Python, MySQL, Power BI, and Power Automate. I successfully automated a digital checklist using Power Automate and SharePoint, which streamlined approvals and minimized errors.

My goal is to innovate and create solutions that not only enhance lives but also drive efficiency through automation. Outside of my professional interests, I enjoy solving puzzles, playing chess, and writing poetry.
